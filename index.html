About Me

I am 22 years of age. I live in Mumbai. I am pursuing a Dual Degree at IIT Bombay: B.Tech in Electrical Engineering and M.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science.

I am a budding Data Scientist and ML engineer. Developing intelligent systems for automation and building end-to-end pipelines for their seamless working interests me quite a lot. My key area of expertise is Python programming for system development, data processing and ML. I have completed multiple projects in Python over the last 4 years.

For my masters thesis, I am working in the field of Music Information Retrieval on a task aimed at finding correspondence between mutiple modalities in Hindustani classical music performances. Through the same I managed to publish a paper at International Society of Music Information Retrival (ISMIR), 2023 in Milan, Italy and am currently working towards a related Journal paper submission. I am also a research assistant at Digital Audio Processing Lab, IIT Bombay. My work has involved extensive amounts of data processing, scripting and pipeline development in Python. I have some background knowledge in Indian Classicial Music which also contributes towards my work.

I am going to start my first full-time job at NoBroker Technologies Solutions Pvt Ltd. in August 2024 as an Associate Data Scientist. The work involves natural language processing and Python development
